About us

Indian Art Villa was founded and started in 2012 by Mr. Virendra Taluka (Managing Director) with a powerful thought of creating wider well being awareness about the multiple health benefits of Copper products. Indian Art Villa is an established utensils manufacturers, exporter and supplier of vast range of products in India which includes traditional copper utensils, Brass ware, Bronze ware, Steel ware, Silver Plated, Spiritual & Feng shui Items. While the concept of storing water in a copper water bottle & Copper Water Pot or other copper vessel arose centuries ago, Indian Art Villa has made a revolution in Copper and Brass ware products, ruling the Home and Kitchen category whilst offering a versatile range along with home décor items as well.
